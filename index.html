About The Artist

The Sound of a New Generation

FlackoB is a Liberian recording artist, songwriter, performer and entertainer currently based in Ghana — one of Africa's most vibrant music capitals. Born and raised in Liberia, he carries the heartbeat of West Africa in every verse, blending the raw energy of his homeland with the polished pulse of contemporary African sound.

His music fuses Afrobeat, Afropop, Hip-Hop, Drill and modern African rhythms into a signature style that speaks to a generation hungry for authenticity. From Monrovia to Accra, FlackoB is building a bridge between cultures — crafting anthems that move dancefloors, touch hearts and put Liberia firmly on the global music map.
